Nokia announced a new series of devices this week - the NSeries, and launched three new high spec phones. Out of the three it was the N91 that really got me excited. It has a 4GB hard drive, GPRS/3G/WiFi, proper headphone socket and a 2 megapixel camera. A spec like that would seriously make me leave my iPod at home. Should be available at the end of 2005.
